Hello, I am Mio Ebisu.

I moved to London in 2018 from Berlin, before Berlin I was born and grown up in Tokyo Japan. Since 2018, I have been living on a narrowboat with my family, my partner, my 1 year + 4months daughter and one black cat. My background is Fine Art, contemporary painting. I studied BA at Musashino Art University in Japan and MA at University of the Arts London. Since 2012, I have been represented by a commercial gallery in Tokyo and I have had many solo exhibitions, group shows, artist residencies, research project granted by Arts Council England. My research theme was making my own paints using raw materials. I am a classic introvert, love painting, thinking and making stuff.

I may sound like a wrong person to be here???Yes, I used to have a complex feeling with technology. I was not good at using Adobe Illustrator like my friends, I was not the neat person. And I blame the society, the technology industry seems very male dominant so I believed I may be not good at because I am women! The things has changed since I started working as LQA, game localization tester. This is full remote job, work from home, the job is very fun, lots of trouble shooting on software, and hardware. We use chat only to communicate to tackle the problem, share the knowledge tips. I loved to think why and how those bugs are occured. To think wide and micro, I am fascinated by those industry.I gained my confidence in technology and problem-solving thinking. And I want to understand the software side bit more to understand what happening underneath.Also, all my collegues are such a fantastic people, smart, fun, and hard working. I want to work with people like them. Also, I know many musician friends who are software engenierrs. I am inspired from them as well. They are in my community, but also work as super technical skill job. I thought because of the opensource culture is very big in software development, the passion people have is not only for money or employment, purely technology and simply love making, and puzzle solving. I like the passion. I found it similar to art that I am attracted to. Once I start learning, I love them. It is like total another universe exists. very different from human world. But whole universe and order in here. then the possiblities of making is just unlimited.
