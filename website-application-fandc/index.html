Hello, I am Mio Ebisu.

About Myself

Hello, my name is Mio Ebisu. I was born and raised in Japan, Tokyo. I moved to Berlin in 2015 and moved to London in 2018.
I live in East London and live on a narrowboat with my family. My partner, 1 year + 4 months daughter and one female black cat.


My background is Fine Art, contemporary painting. I studied BA at Musashino Art University in Japan and MA at University of the Arts London. I have had many solo exhibitions, group shows, artist residencies. Since 2012, I have been represented by a commercial gallery in Tokyo. As well as making paintings, I had a research project that was granted by Arts Council England. My theme was making my own paints using raw materials. I could make paints from stone and plants! I usually use oil paints and acrylic paints as well as making my own. I love painting, thinking and making stuff.

Why Web developer?

I may sound like a wrong person to be interested in Software Engineering??? I used to have a complex feeling with technology. I was not good at using Adobe Illustrator like my friends from art university. I am not the neatest person as I love to be in the messy art studio. Also, I blame the society, the technology industry was very male dominant so I believed I may be not be good at because I am women! But It did not mean, I did not like it. I just did not think I can do it.

Even though I achieved to have regular exhibitions in the super competitive fine art industry, money is always a problem. I needed to have another job to cover pay my bill. I have completely different CV one is for job, one for art. Job CV always looks inconsistent and it does not represent my experience, missing the biggest part but my experience in art is very hard to be recognized to other industry. I wanted to have other technical skills I can develop and more recognizable in job market. So, I decided to go to IT industry.

So, for this my long journey, I tried to get tech related job. Fortunately, I started working as LQA, game localization tester in 2022. This is full remote job, work from home, PC and mobile, wifi router, lots of cables, etc. I realized I am very interested in how system underneath works each other through this job. The fun part of the job, find bugs lots of trouble shooting on software, and hardware. We use chat only to communicate to tackle the problem, share the knowledge tips. I loved to think why and how those bugs are occured. To think wide and micro, I am fascinated by those industry.I gained my confidence in technology and problem-solving thinking. And I want to understand the software side bit more to understand what happening underneath.Also, all my collegues are such a fantastic people, smart, fun, and hard working. I want to work with people like them.

Also, I know many musician friends who are software engenierrs. I am inspired from them as well. They are in my community, but also work as super technical skill job. I thought because of the opensource culture is very big in software development, the passion people have is not only for money or employment, purely technology and simply love making, and puzzle solving. I like the passion. I found it similar to art that I am attracted to. Once I start learning, I love them. It is like total another universe exists. very different from human world. But whole universe and order in here. then the possiblities of making is just unlimited. I love making, so of course I want to become a developer to make and find bugs, be in the community also many job opportunities! The below is my paintings. Here is my website.
