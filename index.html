Sauna in Allotment

Who we are

We built this authentic wood-burning sauna in the community allotment for everyone to enjoy.
Now open to the public. It’s a great place to relax and unwind in a simple, peaceful setting.

There are changing room and public toilets.

There are showers and cold plunges but no hot water! Please bring your own towel and slippers.
